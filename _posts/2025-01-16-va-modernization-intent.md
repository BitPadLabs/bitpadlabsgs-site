As a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business, **BitPadLabs Government Solutions LLC** is uniquely positioned to support the **Department of Veterans Affairs** in its critical mission to serve those who have served our nation.

## Why Veterans Affairs?

The VA serves millions of veterans and their families through healthcare, benefits, and memorial services. Modern, secure, and accessible technology systems are essential to fulfilling this mission effectively.

As veteran leaders ourselves, we understand the importance of reliable systems that deliver for veterans when they need them most.

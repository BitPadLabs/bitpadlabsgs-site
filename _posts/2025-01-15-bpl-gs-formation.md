We are pleased to announce the formation of **BitPadLabs Government Solutions LLC (BPL-GS)**, a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business dedicated to serving federal agencies with modern technology solutions.

## Veteran-Led Excellence

BPL-GS is led by Ryan Burke, a service-disabled veteran who serves as Managing Member and Chief Executive Officer. Our veteran leadership brings a unique perspective and deep commitment to serving those who have served our nation.

## Technical Capabilities

Through our partnership with BitPadLabs LLC as a technical service provider, we deliver world-class capabilities in:

- **DevSecOps & CI/CD:** Secure, automated software delivery pipelines
- **Cloud Modernization:** Infrastructure as Code and cloud-native architectures
- **SAFe & Agile Transformation:** Scaled Agile Framework training and implementation
- **Security & Compliance:** Federal mandate alignment and accessibility standards
- **Federal Experience:** Proven delivery at OPM, CMS, FSA, and SEC

## Our Mission

Our mission is to modernize federal technology systems with a focus on security, scalability, and user-centered design. We bring technical excellence and veteran leadership to every engagement.

## SDVOSB Certification

BPL-GS is certified as a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business through the Small Business Administration, enabling us to support federal agencies through SDVOSB set-aside contracts and partnerships.

## Looking Ahead

We are excited to bring our capabilities to bear on critical federal modernization challenges, particularly in support of the Department of Veterans Affairs and other agencies serving our veteran community.

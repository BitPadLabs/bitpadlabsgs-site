About BitPadLabs Government Solutions

Veteran-Led Excellence in Federal Modernization

BitPadLabs Government Solutions LLC (BPL-GS) is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) dedicated to modernizing technology systems across the federal government, with a focus on serving the Department of Veterans Affairs.

Our Mission

We bring veteran leadership, technical excellence, and deep federal experience to help agencies transform their digital services. Our mission is to deliver secure, scalable, and user-centered solutions that improve the experience for those who serve and have served our nation.

Veteran Leadership

BPL-GS is led by Ryan Burke, a service-disabled veteran who serves as Managing Member and Chief Executive Officer. Our veteran-led structure ensures we understand the unique needs and mission-critical requirements of federal agencies, particularly the Department of Veterans Affairs.

Technical Excellence

Through our relationship with BitPadLabs LLC as a technical service provider, we deliver best-in-class capabilities across:

  • DevSecOps and CI/CD pipeline implementation
  • Cloud migration and infrastructure modernization
  • SAFe and Agile transformation
  • Security compliance and accessibility
  • Federal mandate alignment (M-21-31, M-22-09, SCuBA, FITARA)

Federal Experience

Our team brings extensive experience across multiple federal agencies:

  • Office of Personnel Management (OPM): 2010-2021, 2023-present
  • Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS): 2021-2023
  • Farm Service Agency (FSA): 2025-present
  • Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC): 2025-present

Our Commitment to Veterans Affairs

As an SDVOSB, we are uniquely positioned to support the VA's mission. We understand the critical importance of delivering reliable, secure systems that serve veterans and their families. Our focus on VA modernization drives everything we do—from healthcare IT transformation to benefits platform optimization to compliance with federal accessibility standards.

Certifications & Credentials

  • SDVOSB Certified through the Small Business Administration
  • Team Certifications: GitHub Certified Security Analyst (GCSA), SAFe Program Consultant (SPC), AWS Certified, GitHub Advanced Security
